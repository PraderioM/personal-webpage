My research interests lie in the fields of homotopy theory, homological algebra and representation theory with applications to group theory and related topics. Although I am open to expanding my research horizons. During my PhD I have studied Mackey functors over fusion systems. In particular I have proved an induction result similar to Green correspondence, and my latest work is focused on the study of the sharpness conjecture for fusion systems, which concerns the vanishing of higher limits of the cohomology functor.
