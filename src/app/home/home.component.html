About me

My name is Marco Praderio Bova. I am currently a research fellow in the Oberwolfach Research Institute for Mathematics. Prior to that I was a PhD student in Lancaster University where I did my thesis (titled "Mackey functors over fusion systems") under the supervision of Professor Nadia Mazza.
My research interests lie in the fields of homological algebra, homotopy theory and representation theory with applications to group theory and related topics. Although I am open to expanding my research horizons. During my PhD I have studied Mackey functors over fusion systems. In particular I have proved an induction result similar to Green correspondence, and my latest work is focused on the study of the sharpness conjecture for fusion systems, which concerns the vanishing of higher limits of the cohomology functor.
